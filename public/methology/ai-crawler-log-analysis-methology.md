# AI Crawler Log Analysis Methology

## Core Objective

Define what to prioritize so crawler behavior insights drive stronger index quality, better crawl allocation, and improved technical SEO visibility.

## Primary SEO Ranking Priorities

- Prioritize crawl allocation toward revenue-critical and intent-critical routes.
- Prioritize reduction of low-value crawl waste.
- Prioritize index quality through route-level signal refinement.
- Prioritize recrawl confidence for updated strategic pages.
- Prioritize visibility resilience during content expansion.

## What To Do For Content Direction

- Maintain strategic mapping between high-value pages and crawl priorities.
- Maintain clear service-page importance hierarchy for crawl focus.
- Maintain content freshness signals on conversion-oriented routes.
- Maintain route-level clarity for topic ownership.
- Maintain internal linking logic that supports crawler path efficiency.

## What To Do For Technical SEO Signals

- Prioritize strong discoverability of strategic service and proof pages.
- Prioritize elimination of ambiguous route signals.
- Prioritize canonical consistency for similar topic pages.
- Prioritize structured data presence on high-priority URLs.
- Prioritize index controls for low-value route classes.
- Prioritize sitemap route quality over quantity.

## What To Do For Authority And Trust

- Prioritize transparent quality signals around crawl and index governance.
- Prioritize evidence language around improved discovery and index focus.
- Prioritize reliability narratives for technical SEO process maturity.
- Prioritize consistency between crawl strategy and business-priority pages.

## What To Do For AI Search Relevance

- Prioritize pages with clear topical ownership and concise purpose.
- Prioritize retrieval paths that connect evidence pages to service pages.
- Prioritize explicit context around page role and intent.
- Prioritize machine-readable consistency across crawl-critical templates.

## What To Do For Conversion Intent Alignment

- Prioritize crawl support for pages that influence purchase decisions.
- Prioritize visibility continuity for audit, diagnostics, and implementation pages.
- Prioritize strong internal paths from educational routes to conversion routes.
- Prioritize decision-stage relevance in technical remediation content.

## What To Do For Ongoing Measurement

- Prioritize crawl share on strategic URL groups.
- Prioritize index health trends for core service routes.
- Prioritize recrawl interval quality for frequently updated proof pages.
- Prioritize reduced crawl frequency on low-value pages.
- Prioritize technical lead quality from search sessions.

## Non-Negotiable Focus

- Focus on crawl efficiency linked to business value.
- Focus on index quality over index size.
- Focus on durable technical visibility improvements.
