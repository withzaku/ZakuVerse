Define what to prioritize so SEO and digital growth pages rank for commercial technical-intent queries and drive qualified pipeline.

- Prioritize technical SEO authority themes: crawl quality, index strategy, entity clarity, and conversion intent alignment.
- Prioritize visibility for decision-stage terms around SEO audits, remediation, and growth execution.
- Prioritize service narratives that connect rankings with qualified revenue outcomes.
- Prioritize authority building through benchmark and case-proof content.
- Prioritize search relevance across classic and AI-influenced discovery behaviors.

## What To Do For Content Direction

## What To Do For Technical SEO Signals

- Prioritize canonical stability across related route clusters.
