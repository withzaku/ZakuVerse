# Crawl Budget And Index Hygiene Methology

## Core Objective

Define what to prioritize so crawlers allocate resources to strategic URLs and indexing quality stays concentrated on business-critical pages.

## Web Research Signals Used

- Prioritize crawl accessibility and clear index intent on valuable routes.
- Prioritize duplicate reduction and canonical consolidation.
- Prioritize route organization that reflects topic structure.
- Prioritize crawler-friendly response behavior and freshness signals.
- Prioritize cache and recrawl clarity for updated content.

## Primary Ranking Priorities

- Prioritize high-value service and case routes for crawl focus.
- Prioritize reduction of low-value duplicate and thin URL variants.
- Prioritize stable canonical ownership for key topics.
- Prioritize directory and route structures that communicate topical grouping.
- Prioritize crawl efficiency during large content expansion.

## What To Do For Content Direction

- Maintain clear topical ownership per route cluster.
- Maintain one primary page per commercial intent target.
- Maintain distinct value propositions across related URLs.
- Maintain strategic freshness on high-value pages only.

## What To Do For Technical SEO Signals

- Prioritize canonical consistency for similar and overlapping pages.
- Prioritize redirect discipline for deprecated and merged URLs.
- Prioritize robots and indexing controls on low-value routes.
- Prioritize sitemap quality aligned to index-worthy pages.
- Prioritize caching and freshness signals that support efficient recrawling.

## What To Do For Authority And Trust

- Prioritize clean index presentation with minimal ambiguity.
- Prioritize reliability in URL ownership and page purpose.
- Prioritize trust through stable, predictable route governance.

## What To Do For AI Search Relevance

- Prioritize high-quality index surfaces for retrieval systems.
- Prioritize clear page intent to reduce citation ambiguity.
- Prioritize strong internal pathways from authority pages to service pages.

## What To Do For Ongoing Measurement

- Prioritize crawl-share trends for strategic URL groups.
- Prioritize index coverage quality on conversion routes.
- Prioritize duplicate cluster reduction over time.
- Prioritize recrawl response on frequently updated proof pages.

## Non-Negotiable Focus

- Focus on index quality, not index volume.
- Focus on crawl allocation tied to business value.
- Focus on stable canonical ownership.
