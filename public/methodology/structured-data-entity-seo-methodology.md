# Structured Data And Entity SEO Methodology

## Core Objective

Define what to prioritize so structured data improves search understanding, rich-result eligibility, and entity clarity across high-value pages.

## Web Research Signals Used

- Prioritize structured data as explicit meaning signals for search engines.
- Prioritize JSON-LD maintainability and scale readiness.
- Prioritize complete and accurate properties over excessive incomplete markup.
- Prioritize structured data that matches visible on-page content.
- Prioritize ongoing validation and post-deployment monitoring.

## Primary Ranking Priorities

- Prioritize entity clarity for brand, service, and case-study relationships.
- Prioritize rich-result eligibility where supported and relevant.
- Prioritize schema consistency across key page templates.
- Prioritize signal quality for commercial-intent pages.
- Prioritize semantic alignment between metadata and schema claims.

## What To Do For Content Direction

- Maintain schema-aware content sections with clear topical ownership.
- Maintain consistent naming for services, offerings, and proof assets.
- Maintain explicit relationship language between pages and entities.
- Maintain trust-centric statements grounded in measurable outcomes.

## What To Do For Technical SEO Signals

- Prioritize structured data coverage on all conversion-critical routes.
- Prioritize completeness of required and high-value recommended properties.
- Prioritize validity monitoring for template and deployment changes.
- Prioritize canonical and schema coherence across related pages.
- Prioritize crawl-accessible pages with stable schema signals.

## What To Do For Authority And Trust

- Prioritize entity consistency across all major route clusters.
- Prioritize schema statements that align with visible evidence.
- Prioritize authoritative representation of services and capabilities.
- Prioritize durable signal quality over schema volume inflation.

## What To Do For AI Search Relevance

- Prioritize machine-readable context for retrieval and citation systems.
- Prioritize disambiguation signals for closely related service themes.
- Prioritize concise semantic alignment between page intent and schema intent.

## What To Do For Ongoing Measurement

- Prioritize rich-result visibility trends where applicable.
- Prioritize schema validation health by route cluster.
- Prioritize click-through behavior on schema-enhanced pages.
- Prioritize regression detection after template updates.

## Non-Negotiable Focus

- Focus on valid, accurate, and visible-content-aligned schema.
- Focus on entity coherence across the site.
- Focus on maintainable structured data at scale.
