# Technical SEO Operator Checklist (Local Services)

Use this checklist for local service businesses with location and service-area routes.

## Core Focus

- Prioritize crawl efficiency for location-service pages with conversion intent.
- Reduce index bloat from thin near-duplicate area pages.
- Ensure local trust routes (contact, reviews, case studies) are crawl-accessible.

## Remediation Steps

- Merge weak location variants into stronger regional hubs when appropriate.
- Normalize canonical and structured data signals for local pages.
- Improve internal links between service, location, and proof pages.

## Validation

- Track crawl revisit rate on top location-service pages.
- Monitor index quality of local pages and duplicate suppression.
- Compare qualified local lead submissions before and after fixes.
